Production Project Topic:
Enhancing Competition Designs through Exploration of Alternative Design Techniques and Materials
Students from the Diploma of Vocational Education (Fashion) participated in the 5th「別出『深』裁時裝設計比賽2025」competition held on 6 September 2025.
時裝文憑學生參加了於2025年9月6日舉行的第五屆「別出『深』裁時裝設計比賽2025」。
You may need to find and buy new materials that you did not use before.
你可能需要尋找及購買之前沒有用過的新材料。
Choose one design from the competition that you want to improve.
從比賽中選擇一個你想改進的設計。
This event was organised by the Sham Shui Po District Youth Development and Civic Education Committee and the Asian New Generation Creativity Design Association.
此活動由深水埗區青年發展及公民教育委員會和亞洲新生代創意設計協會合辦。
Review your original competition design and decide what you want to change, improve or enhance.
檢視你原本的比賽設計，並決定你想改變、改進或加強的地方。
Some design techniques may take time to learn and practise.
某些設計技巧可能需要時間學習和練習。
After the competition, you have a chance to work on your design.
比賽後，你有機會繼續改進你的設計。
Look for different materials available in the market.
尋找市場上可用的不同材料。
